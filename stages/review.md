The daily verification stage. The tool quizzes you on flashcards based on the Ebbinghaus forgetting curve. It enforces a hard rule: you must type your answer in the terminal. The agent grades your input (tolerant of typos, catching the meaning) and overwrites the intervals in the database accordingly.
